## Executive Summary

The Shivshakti Gurukulam website is a single-page application showcasing a traditional Indian martial arts training institution based in Nagpur, Maharashtra. The website serves as the primary digital presence for the foundation, enabling prospective students to learn about programs, view galleries, and register for training.
