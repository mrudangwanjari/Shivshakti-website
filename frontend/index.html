Shivshakti Gurukulam

शिवशक्ती गुरुकुलम् — प्राचीन परंपरा, आधुनिक दृष्टी

Shivshakti Gurukulam is a training centre dedicated to preserving ancient Indian martial arts, Akhada culture, and holistic physical-mental development.

Drawing inspiration from the valour of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, we instil courage, discipline, and patriotism in the youth — especially empowering women to reclaim their strength.

Our Programs

Training Disciplines

प्रशिक्षण कार्यक्रम

⚔️
Talwarbaazi
तलवारबाजी

The art of the sword — rooted in Maratha warrior tradition. Students learn classical sword forms, combat footwork, and the philosophy of the blade.

🪃
Dandpatta
दांडपट्टा

The legendary gauntlet sword of the Marathas. An intensely dynamic discipline requiring strength, agility, and body coordination.

🏹
Bhala / स्पेअर
भाला प्रशिक्षण

Ancient spear fighting techniques that develop reach, balance, and explosive power. One of the oldest martial disciplines in India.

🏹
Bana
बाण

The ancient art of archery. Master precision, focus, and the warrior's discipline through traditional bow and arrow training.

💫
Chakra Vidya
चक्र युद्धकला

The rare and ancient art of the war-disc. Precision, spin mechanics, and target training in this almost-lost Indian martial form.

🪵
Lathi-Kathi
लाठी-काठी

Staff combat — the martial art of the common warrior. Develops coordination, speed, and endurance through rhythmic stick forms.

🧘
Yoga
योग

Foundation of all martial arts. Traditional Yoga, Pranayama, and Surya Namaskar to build the warrior's body and calm mind.

Women Empowerment

Shakti — The Warrior Woman

महिला सशक्तीकरण — शक्ती, साहस, स्वावलंबन

At Shivshakti Gurukulam, women are not just welcome — they are at the centre of our mission. We believe every woman carries the spirit of Jijabai and Lakshmibai within her.

Our women's programs go beyond physical training — they build unshakeable confidence, situational awareness, and the will to stand up for oneself and others.
