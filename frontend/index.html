Our Story

Shivshakti Gurukulam

"Shastrena Rakshite Rashtr, Rashtr Chinta Pravartate"

(Only in a nation protected by arms can knowledge and wisdom flourish)

Peace cannot be established without strength and preparedness—this timeless truth is reflected in the strategies of Lord Krishna in the Mahabharata, the Arthashastra of Chanakya, and the military brilliance of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

India has been the birthplace of ancient martial traditions since time immemorial. Over time, these martial arts evolved across regions, adapting to local cultures while preserving their original essence.

🛡️ Our Legacy

Shivshakti Gurukulam, since 1870, has been dedicated to preserving and promoting India's ancient martial arts and heritage.

Our key activities include:
• Conducting martial arts training camps across Maharashtra and India
• Organizing exhibitions of historical weapons and coins in schools and colleges
• Creating awareness about Indian history and heritage
• Preserving traditional combat techniques

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the youth by:
• Building physical and mental strength
• Providing self-defense training
• Instilling awareness of Indian culture and history
• Developing disciplined, courageous, and patriotic individuals

⚔️ Our Initiatives

We organize various impactful and educational programs:
🏰 Fort Exploration Campaigns
⚔️ Battle Heritage Studies
🚶‍♂️ Nagpur Heritage Walk
🛡️ Martial Arts Training Centers

We also work in areas like:
Spiritual Development • Environmental Awareness • De-addiction Campaigns • Entrepreneurship • Social Responsibility • Women Empowerment • Caring for Historical Statues

🇮🇳 Our Philosophy

History teaches us that when a society becomes disarmed, it risks losing its culture and identity. Knowledge without strength is incomplete, and strength without knowledge is dangerous.

We believe today's youth must understand the true meaning of peace and the strength required to protect it.

Our Programs

Training Disciplines

प्रशिक्षण कार्यक्रम

⚔️
Talwarbaazi
तलवारबाजी

The art of the sword — rooted in Maratha warrior tradition. Students learn classical sword forms, combat footwork, and the philosophy of the blade.

🪃
Dandpatta
दांडपट्टा

The legendary gauntlet sword of the Marathas. An intensely dynamic discipline requiring strength, agility, and body coordination.

🏹
Bhala / स्पेअर
भाला प्रशिक्षण

Ancient spear fighting techniques that develop reach, balance, and explosive power. One of the oldest martial disciplines in India.

🏹
Bana
बाण

The ancient art of archery. Master precision, focus, and the warrior's discipline through traditional bow and arrow training.

💫
Chakra Vidya
चक्र युद्धकला

The rare and ancient art of the war-disc. Precision, spin mechanics, and target training in this almost-lost Indian martial form.

🪵
Lathi-Kathi
लाठी-काठी

Staff combat — the martial art of the common warrior. Develops coordination, speed, and endurance through rhythmic stick forms.

🧘
Yoga
योग

Foundation of all martial arts. Traditional Yoga, Pranayama, and Surya Namaskar to build the warrior's body and calm mind.

Shakti — The Warrior Woman

महिला सशक्तीकरण — शक्ती, साहस, स्वावलंबन

At Shivshakti Gurukulam, women are not just welcome — they are at the centre of our mission. We believe every woman carries the spirit of Jijabai and Lakshmibai within her.

Our women's programs go beyond physical training — they build unshakeable confidence, situational awareness, and the will to stand up for oneself and others.
